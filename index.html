How it Works

1.

Log into Spotify

We use Spotify's secure API to connect your account with Moodify. Moodify requests permission to access only your "Top" songs. Read about our privacy policy below.

2.

View Your Top Songs

Spotify creates a list of your "Top" songs, which we display to you. Moodify does not create this list itself, we just use Spotify's.

3.

Select Moods

For each song, use the sliders to adjust the different mood levels to represent how the song makes you feel. Once you've done this for all songs, click GET RECOMMENDATIONS.

4.

Get Recommended Songs

Adjust the sliders to represent the type of song you currently wish to hear. Click SUBMIT and we'll create a small playlist of songs catered to your tastes and mood. Listen to the songs and let us know if you enjoyed our selections.

Get Started!

Privacy

We use Spotify's secure API to log into your account. The only data we request access for is your Spotify "Top" songs list, a list Spotify creates for each user. Likewise internally, we only store data on the songs themselves and the moods you assign to each song. No pesonal information or account data is kept at all.
