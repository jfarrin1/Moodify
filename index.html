How it Works

1.

Log into Spotify

We use Spotify's secure API to connect your account with Moodify. Moodify requests permission to access only your public playlists. Read about our privacy policy below.

2.

Select Mood

We'll display your top 10 songs from 2017 and you just have to select a mood for each song.

3.

Get your Recommended Songs

Select your current mood and click Submit. Then listen to our recommended songs catered specifically for you.

4.

Give Feedback

If our recommendations don't match your current mood and musical tastes we would love to know so our algorithm can improve. Select a different mood for any recommended song and click Give Feedback.
